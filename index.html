Destinations

Aethel (Ah-thel)

Nestled within a binary star system, Aethel boasts perpetual twilight skies painted with vibrant auroras. This temperate world offers lush bioluminescent flora and fauna, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and astrophotographers. Explore crystal caverns, witness bioluminescent waterfalls, and relax on beaches bathed in the soft glow of two suns.

Zephyr (Zef-ir)

A gas giant with swirling storms of vibrant hues, Zephyr offers a breathtaking spectacle for the adventurous traveler. Take a controlled descent through the atmosphere on a specialized craft, witnessing the ever-changing cloud formations and witnessing lightning storms unlike any on Earth. After the descent, relax in a luxurious orbital habitat, enjoying panoramic views of the swirling gassy maelstrom below.

Xylia (Zy-lee-ah)

An ocean world shrouded in perpetual mist, Xylia offers a glimpse into a watery alien ecosystem. Glide through the ethereal mists in a submersible craft, encountering bioluminescent creatures and exploring underwater volcanic vents teeming with life. For the truly intrepid, explore ancient ruins submerged deep within the ocean, remnants of a long-lost civilization.
